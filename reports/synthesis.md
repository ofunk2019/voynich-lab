## 1. What This Project Is, and Is Not

A statistical-analysis laboratory for the Voynich manuscript (Beinecke MS 408)
that (a) reproduces the text's known statistical signature, (b) implements
hypothesis families as text generators, and (c) ranks them by measured fidelity to
that signature against control corpora. **It translates nothing and claims no
meaning** (VOY-DOC-01): its verdicts are distances, rankings, and exclusions,
including negative results documented at the same level as positive ones
(VOY-DOC-05).

## 4. T2 - Controls: What "Nothing" Looks Like

Six corpora with the same layout skeleton (30,525 tokens each), one
battery. Aggregate distance to Voynichese (group z-scores, 15 metrics):

| Rank | Control | Distance |
| ---: | --- | ---: |
| 1 | Shuffled Voynich | 0.976 |
| 2 | Latin | 1.681 |
| 3 | Enciphered Latin | 1.681 |
| 4 | Gibberish | 1.691 |
| 5 | Self-citation | 1.887 |

Central lesson, proven by measurement: clear Latin and Latin under
monoalphabetic substitution have **identical metric-by-metric distances**. A
simple cipher moves no structural statistic. If Voynichese were a simple cipher of
a European language, it would have that language's statistics. It does not. This
is the project's strongest exclusion.

The leading family, **structured self-citation**, embodies: copying from the
lines above (Timm & Schinner), slot-replacement mutations (prefix/core/suffix),
and a small closed lexicon of "function words" weighted by 1/rank. Each ingredient
was validated by a dedicated diagnostic on the working corpus:

- `reports/diag-selfcitation.md`: local copying fails under random edits
  (length drift, over-similarity). The edit model was guilty, not locality.
  Major methodological fallout: single-draw scoring rewarded seed luck, so every
  score is now averaged.
- `reports/diag-zipf.md`: exact reuse (Simon process) repairs the Zipf slope but
  starves hapax; head and tail are coupled in this mechanism. Documented
  structural negative.
- `reports/diag-lexicon.md`: the closed lexicon decouples: Zipf AND hapax are held
  together for the first time, and global reuse becomes unnecessary
  (`pGlobalReuse=0` for all winners).

## 7. T5 - Structure Above the Line: Is There a Message? (Phase Closed)

The four planned measures converge (`reports/structure.md`), and the central
result is a **bracketing**, not a proof. At matched distance, vocabulary contrast
by section (flat with distance) and paragraph cohesion exceed every content-free
writing drift tested - folio order, writing campaigns by section or by hand - but
stay below a deliberate, complete redraw of word stocks per section/paragraph: a
content-free mechanism covers the observed value. The most directly referential
test - do **labels** behave like names of their drawings? - is negative: their
co-occurrence with their own page's text sits inside the stratified-permutation
null (p about 0.27). Paragraph-edge habits (gallows, longer first line) are real
but mechanical.

**Conclusion (VOY-DOC-01/05):** nothing above the line REQUIRES semantics. This is
not proof that the text is meaningless - it shifts the burden of proof: any content
claim must now explain why meaning left none of the traces we looked for. The
remaining frontier is external (section 9): meaning lives between a text and a
world, never inside the text alone.

## 8. What We Know, What We Do Not Know

**Established (with reports):**
1. The manuscript's published signature is reproduced by our instruments and is
   robust to a transcriber change.
2. The "simple substitution over a European language" family is excluded by
   measurement.
3. The abjad (vowel-stripped Latin) and Naibbe-style verbose cipher, as
   implemented, generalize poorly (overfitting measured on held-out).
4. A meaningless mechanical process, structured local copying plus closed lexicon,
   is the family most compatible with the measured signature.
5. Above the line (T5), section/paragraph vocabulary organization is bracketed by
   content-free mechanisms and labels are non-referential: nothing there requires
   a message either.

**Honest limits:**
1. "Compatible" does not mean "this is how the manuscript was produced"
   (VOY-DOC-01); a ranking is not a historical demonstration.
2. The leading family consumes real corpus fragments (documented information
   asymmetry; its synthetic variants remain the best at equal information, but
   farther away).
3. Fine line effects and the residual frontier remain unreproduced.
4. The held-out (26 pages) is small: final evaluations are
   noisy (sd displayed) and deliberately few (log kept).
5. T5 is a bracketing, not a disproof of meaning: a partial stock variation - still
   content-free - covers the observed structure. Only external anchoring (T6) could
   go further.
